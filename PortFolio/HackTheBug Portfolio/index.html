Capture The Flag

Capture the Flag (CTF) is a cybersecurity competition or game where participants compete to solve a series of challenges to obtain flags. These flags are typically short strings of text or unique identifiers that represent a particular goal or accomplishment within the game. CTF challenges are designed to test various skills and knowledge in areas such as cryptography, reverse engineering, web security, binary exploitation, forensics, network analysis, and more. Participants often work in teams or individually to solve these challenges within a specified time frame.
